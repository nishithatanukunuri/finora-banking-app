Banking, Simplified. Here's How.

Getting started with Finora is fast, secure, and can be done entirely from your phone. Follow these simple steps to take control of your financial future.

A Three-Step Journey to Financial Freedom

1. Sign Up in Minutes

Create your account securely from our website or mobile app. Our paperless process is designed to be quick and hassle-free, getting you set up in less time than it takes to brew a coffee.

2. Fund Your Account

Easily add money to your new Finora account. You can set up a direct deposit from your employer, link an external bank account for seamless transfers, or deposit a check using your phone's camera.

3. Manage, Save, & Grow

Once funded, you can manage your money with our powerful tools. Track spending, set up automatic savings goals, invest commission-free, and use our AI-powered insights to make smarter decisions.
