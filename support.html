Finora Help Center

Find answers to common questions below. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

How do I open an account?

Opening an account is simple! Just click the "Sign Up" button, fill out the required information, and verify your identity. The whole process takes less than 5 minutes.

Are my funds secure and FDIC insured?

Yes, your security is our top priority. All data is encrypted, and your deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000 through our partner banks.

What are the fees associated with a Finora account?

Our Basic and Standard plans have no monthly maintenance fees or minimum balance requirements. Our Premium plan has a monthly fee that unlocks advanced features. You can find all the details on our Pricing page.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. We'll send you an email with instructions on how to securely reset it.
